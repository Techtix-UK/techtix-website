The Techtix Story

Techtix is a Midhurst-based business founded and run by 14-year-old Adam. Yes, you read that right—a 14-year-old with a massive passion for technology, innovation, and helping businesses grow.

What started as a deep interest in the digital world has quickly evolved into a cross-continental operation bridging the UK and South Africa. Despite the young leadership, Techtix delivers mature, premium solutions ranging from proactive technical support to remote Airbnb management.

We believe that innovation isn't bound by age. It's driven by curiosity, dedication, and the willingness to power tomorrow's solutions today. When you work with Techtix, you're partnering with a team that brings fresh perspectives and relentless energy to every project.
